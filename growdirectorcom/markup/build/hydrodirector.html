If you want your plants to thrive, you must give them the right amount of light and spectrum. Too much or too little light — or the wrong spectrum — can harm your plants and make it difficult for them to grow. With a DimmerDirector, you can be sure your plants are getting just the right amount of light and spectrum over a certain period of time.
